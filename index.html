About

A little about me.

Tiffany Ngan is a medical student based in Hong Kong, pursuing medicine with focus, warmth, and genuine intellectual curiosity. With an international background and a deeply empathetic nature, she brings both rigour and humanity to everything she does.

She is drawn to the human side of medicine — the stories behind symptoms, the relationships built in clinic rooms, and the profound responsibility of showing up for people at their most vulnerable. While she has yet to settle on a specialty, she is exploring her path with open eyes and real enthusiasm.

Outside of medicine, Tiffany is a reader, a traveller at heart, and someone who believes the best doctors are first and foremost thoughtful people.
